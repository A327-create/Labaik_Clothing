About Our Company

About Us & Our Skills

Labaik Aqsa is an apparel brand based in Dubai, dedicated to blending fashion with purpose. We specialize in creating high-quality clothing that supports Palestinian communities and survivors of genocide. Our brand embodies confidence and functionality, merging modern style with a commitment to social impact. By providing garments that are both stylish and meaningful, we aim to empower individuals while contributing to the greater cause of supporting the Muslim Ummah and fostering positive change.

Made in UAE

Labaik Aqsa clothing, a brand under MBR Apparel based in Dubai, is a leading clothes manufacturing business catering to both Business to Business and Business to Consumer. We embody the essence of professional efficacy and effortless productivity. Our brand exudes confidence, reliability, and functionality, reflecting a seamless fusion of style and utility in every garment. We inspire a sense of empowerment, enabling individuals to tackle challenges head-on while feeling comfortable, stylish, and ultimately fulfilled

Sustainability

At Labaik Aqsa, we are dedicated to environmental stewardship and ethical practices. Our garments are crafted with sustainable materials and processes, ensuring they are both impactful and kind to the planet. We emphasize recyclable fabrics and responsible production methods to reduce our ecological impact while supporting Palestinian communities through every piece of clothing.

Vission

To set a global standard in ethical fashion by using our platform to support Palestinian communities and uplift survivors through impactful and stylish apparel.

Mission

To produce high-quality, contemporary clothing that supports Palestinian causes, committed to sustainability and ethical practices, making each garment a part of a greater mission.
